# Bialik Secondary School

The **private Jewish high school** in Montreal that [[people/robby-hoffman|Robby]] attended on subsidy as a teenager. The "rich Jewish private school" she names in nearly every long-form interview — without naming the school by name in any compiled raw transcript so far.

## Key Takeaways

- **Named in Wikipedia.** Verified via the school's [Wikipedia article on Bialik Secondary School](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bialik_Secondary_School).
- **Location:** Côte Saint-Luc, Montreal.
- **Robby's status:** subsidized. Three or so kids in her grade were on subsidy and were reminded of it constantly.
- **The school is the setting for** several of Robby's recurring stories: [[bits/package-deal|"It's a package deal"]] (grandfather's interview line), the long-tunic + turtleneck uniform self-positioning as the "religious-weird-Ellie-Kemper-of-the-school," the *"can't you just be good?"* school-discipline arc, and the field-trip-bus extra-charges her mother declined ([[themes/money]]).
- **Connected to:** Robby's outing scene in [[themes/queerness|her queerness page]] (the cafeteria walk after the bathroom-makeout was at Bialik or a successor college — sources are ambiguous about whether the outing happened in late high school or first year of McGill).
