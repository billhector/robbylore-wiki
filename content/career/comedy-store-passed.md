# Passed at the Comedy Store

Getting **"passed"** at LA's Hollywood **Comedy Store** is a benchmark for working comedians — it means the club's bookers have approved you as a regular spot. [[people/robby-hoffman|Robby]] got passed in **April 2025**.

## Key Takeaways

- **Robby was not passed at the Comedy Store for years** despite assuming she was — she had been working spots and didn't realize she was running them as guest, not as a passed regular. Per [[people/jordan-jensen|Jordan Jensen]]'s Bein' Ian Ep. 115 (Oct 2024): Robby learned this from **Emily** (booker): *"Emily just spoke to me — I should be showcasing. I always thought when we're in that little room that it is a showcase room."*
- **Also not passed at the Comedy Cellar** (New York) at the time.
- **Adam, the booker at Mothership** (Austin), had been her first home-club pass; Robby was working there reliably through 2024.
- **April 2025: officially passed at the Comedy Store.** Anniewood Ep. 127 (Annie Lederman) opens with Annie introducing Robby as *"newly passed to the Comedy Store"* — the episode's framing announcement.
- The delay sat alongside Robby's bigger TV/Netflix wins (*Hacks* Emmy nom, *Wake Up* production) — she had Hollywood success but the live-comedy gatekeeping arrived later.

## Why it matters

A signature [[themes/money|class-aware]] career detail: Robby achieved national TV recognition before the local LA stand-up infrastructure formally certified her. By the time she got the Store, she didn't need it for visibility — but she still wanted it, and the delay had been mostly invisible to outsiders.
