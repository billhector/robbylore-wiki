# HBO Script Sneak

Early in her career, [[people/robby-hoffman|Robby]] **snuck a script into HBO Canada** by impersonating a courier. The story is one of her most-told.

## Key Takeaways

- Refused to pay $4.99 for Final Draft; wrote in Word + Courier 12 to fake the formatting.
- Hand-delivered to HBO Canada in a stolen manila envelope, posing as a courier.
- Got a $1,000 option — bought one beer to celebrate.
- The [[themes/money|class-translation]] story Robby tells when explaining her hustle muscle.

## How it worked

- Robby refused to spend **$4.99 on Final Draft** screenwriting software.
- Wrote the script in **Microsoft Word, in Courier 12**, formatted to look like Final Draft output.
- **Stole manila envelopes from KPMG** ("they had all the good supplies… Shout out to the accounting firm who hired me. I appreciate you.") — $5K worth of supplies, in her telling — and used KPMG's printer to print five or six copies.
- Hand-delivered the script to HBO Canada presenting as a courier: *"the president of HBO asked to read this."* Security said *"We don't have a president."* Robby: *"I don't know. I'm just a courier. They asked me to get this to the HBO whatever."*
- Got an option — **$1,000** from HBO Canada. The script never got made but circulated, and got Robby her first rep.
- **This is a separate sale from the later HBO US sale, [[projects/unentitled|*Unentitled*]]**, which she sold post-[[career/wga-strike-speech|WGA speech]].

## Robby's reflection on it

- *"I just decided to print my script at work for free where we had very nice printing room and staplers and whatever."*
- *"I can't believe it worked."* — Tom Power on Q, 2025.
- The clearest articulation of Robby's *"the system has cheap rails if you know how to ride them"* posture, which extends straight to the [[career/wga-strike-speech|WGA-strike speech]] (where she questioned the union's spending on real estate instead of lawyers).

## Why it matters

- The script-sneak is the [[themes/money|class-translation]] story Robby tells when she wants to explain her hustle muscle.
- It also frames her later **[[career/wga-strike-speech|WGA-strike skepticism]]** — Robby is openly suspicious of the entertainment industry's gatekeeping and pricing structures.
