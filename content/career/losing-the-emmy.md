# Losing the Emmy

[[people/robby-hoffman|Robby]] was Emmy-nominated for her role in [[projects/hacks|Hacks]] in 2025 — for **six lines of dialogue across the season**. She lost. Her sister **[[people/kaya-hoffman|Kaya]]** had flown in from Scottsdale for the ceremony.

## What happened

- Robby and Kaya at the ceremony.
- Robby's category called; Robby lost.
- Kaya stood up and marched her out, on-the-spot: *"We don't need this shit. I came all the way from Arizona for this."*
- Robby came back later and **still presented the Cinematography Emmy** that same night.

## Why it matters

- The "six lines and an Emmy nomination" is now one of Robby's signature credentialing bits in interviews.
- Kaya's reaction — the older sister yanking the younger one out — has become a Hoffman-family folklore line.
