# McDonald's Job

[[people/robby-hoffman|Robby]] worked at **McDonald's** as a teenager in Montreal. The job is the source of a small library of bits she still uses.

## Greatest hits

- **The walk-in fridge refusal:** *"This Jew will not be going into that fridge."* Refused to enter the cold storage. Asked a coworker to retrieve buns instead.
- **The apple-pie-throwing incident:** A customer demanding fries got an apple pie launched at them.
- **The McFlurry sabotage:** Robby swapped the Oreo McFlurry mix-in for **Smarties and chocolate sauce** for a personal mix.
- **The black-socks discipline:** Lunch-rush hygiene-pin moment — she was the only one in proper black socks.
- **Worked with friend Sheni.**

## Why it matters

- Robby's [[themes/money|class translation]] reads back to the McDonald's job — she still orders the **Number 7** (two cheeseburger meal) when traveling, including alone.
