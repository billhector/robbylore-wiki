# McDonald's Job

[[people/robby-hoffman|Robby]] worked at **McDonald's** as a teenager in Montreal. The job is the source of a small library of bits she still uses.

## Key Takeaways

- Teenage Montreal McDonald's gig is the well behind multiple recurring bits (fridge refusal, apple-pie throw, McFlurry sabotage).
- Reads as Robby's working-class apprenticeship — the [[themes/money|class translation]] anchor for her stand-up.
- Still orders the **Number 7** (two-cheeseburger meal) when traveling alone — the job left a brand-loyalty residue.

## Greatest hits

- **The walk-in fridge refusal:** *"This Jew will not be going into that fridge."* Refused to enter the cold storage. Asked a coworker to retrieve buns instead.
- **The apple-pie-throwing incident:** A customer demanding fries got an apple pie launched at them.
- **The McFlurry sabotage:** Robby swapped the Oreo McFlurry mix-in for **Smarties and chocolate sauce** for a personal mix.
- **The black-socks discipline:** Lunch-rush hygiene-pin moment — she was the only one in proper black socks.
- **Worked with friend Sheni.**
- **The 12-to-8 shift:** Robby + Sheni picked the noon-to-eight slot so they could sleep in and go out after. They were friends with the manager who had the keys; he'd open the restaurant and cook them fries after a night of drinking.
- **Take-home Diet Cokes:** Robby and Sheni would each take home two of the biggest soft-drink cups of Diet Coke at the end of every shift.
- **Half-off-food perk:** the main reason Robby ate there twice a day every shift.
- **Broke kosher at McDonald's, age 19.** Robby was [[themes/orthodox-upbringing|kosher till 19]]; she broke it on an **Egg McMuffin** at the mall McDonald's across the street from a McGill 8am exam she'd finished early. *"I was so starving. There was no kosher food anywhere. I wonder if they still have breakfast?"* Living on her own by then, didn't tell family.
- **40th-birthday catering (March 2024).** Per Bless These Braces / Tam: Robby catered her own 40th birthday party with McDonald's — 40 cheeseburgers, 15 fries, 60 nuggets, all sauces. *"You get to a part of this, McDonald's, they shut it down."* Side effect: apartment smelled like a fart for days.

## Why it matters

- Robby's [[themes/money|class translation]] reads back to the McDonald's job — she still orders the **Number 7** (two cheeseburger meal) when traveling, including alone.
