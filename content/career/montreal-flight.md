# Montreal Flight (from Florida)

The foundational [[themes/family|Hoffman family]] origin event — first told publicly by [[people/robby-hoffman|Robby]] on [[projects/talk-easy|Talk Easy with Sam Fragoso]] in December 2025.

## What happened

- Robby's family was in **Florida** because her grandfather Zadie (Nathan Lubovski) had offered her father a job.
- The father was **drinking heavily, studying religion intensely, and abusive.**
- Robby's mother had a restraining order put in place.
- **Grandfather Zadie** flew to Florida with his best friend, **Germaine** — a French-Canadian former cop. They arrived in **two cabs.**
- They extracted Robby's mother and the ten children.
- The family flew to **Montreal in February.** Snow was, in Robby's framing, "up to here."
- **[[people/uncle-eddie|Great-uncle Eddie]]** picked them up at the airport.
- The plan was temporary; the stay became permanent.

## Why it matters

- This is the moment that determines everything downstream: [[themes/orthodox-upbringing|Lubavitch Montreal]], one bathroom for twelve people, [[themes/canada|the Canadian career start]], [[career/accounting-to-standup|KPMG → comedy]].
- Robby noted on Talk Easy that this was the **first time she'd told this story publicly.**
