# Vegas Elopement (2025)

[[people/robby-hoffman|Robby]] and [[people/gabby-windey|Gabby Windey]] **eloped in Las Vegas** in January 2025, after the LA fires displaced them from home.

## What happened

- **Displaced from LA** by the January 2025 wildfires.
- Stayed in a Wynn hotel suite that "looked like a wedding suite."
- Robby proposed via [[bits/proposing-via-crossword|the custom crossword]] she'd commissioned in Toronto.
- **Wedding package:** $799 chapel package.
- **Officiant:** **Reverend Nature**, in Yeezys.
- **Engagement-night dinner:** Wynn all-you-can-eat buffet. Crab legs.

## Why it matters

- Anchors Robby's [[themes/marriage|pro-marriage]] position with a [[themes/money|class-aware]] cheap-as-possible execution — the most expensive part of the wedding was Gabby herself.
