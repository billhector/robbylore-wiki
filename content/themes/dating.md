# Dating

A favorite [[people/robby-hoffman|Robby]] theme: dating mechanics, viewed from the post-married vantage. She's been married to [[people/gabby-windey|Gabby]] since 2025 but the dating material keeps appearing as career advice for the next generation.

## Key Takeaways

- **Jewish-school-slutty:** Robby gave six (give or take) hand jobs in Orthodox high school. Two distinct boys total.
- **Pull-rich-men playbook** — Robby's tutorial framing of how growing up poor + Lubavitch is actually elite training for dating wealthy partners. See [[bits/pull-rich-men]].
- **["One hot, one smart"]([[bits/one-hot-one-smart]])** rule — every relationship needs one of each, divvied however.
- **First-date best practices** Robby endorses:
  - Card down *before* bathroom break.
  - Always offer to pay; insist on it if you're "the boy in the relationship."
  - Kiss in the car move beats the doorstep move.
- **Pre-Gabby relationships** sound thin in current sources; Robby positions the Gabby relationship as the one she was practicing for.
- *"What are you, [[bits/dora-the-explorer|Dora the Explorer]]?"* — her first line to Gabby was a riff on Gabby's self-description as "exploring."
