# Marriage

[[people/robby-hoffman|Robby]] married **[[people/gabby-windey|Gabby Windey]]** in [[career/vegas-elopement|Vegas]] in 2025, with the **$799 chapel package** at the Wynn after the LA fires displaced them. The proposal was [[bits/proposing-via-crossword|a custom crossword puzzle]] built by David Kaplan in Toronto.

## Key Takeaways

- **Eloped Vegas** during the [[career/vegas-elopement|LA fire displacement]] of January 2025. $799 package, Reverend Nature in Yeezys. Engagement-night dinner: Wynn all-you-can-eat crab leg buffet. Gabby's framing: *"If everything burns down, I just want to be married to you."* Robby: *"All right."*
- **Separate bathrooms.** *"Gab and I do separate bathrooms because she explodes in the bathroom — serums and potions — and I have, like, one toothbrush."*
- **No kids planned.** *"Gab and I, we're not having kids, and we like to go for dinner. We date as much as humanly possible, and when we're together, we live in restaurants."*
- **Gift-giving:** Robby gave Gabby an early edition of Sylvia Plath's *The Bell Jar*. Gabby got into reading "maybe two years ago" — Robby plans to build a collection with one book a year.
- **Crossword proposal:** Gabby thought the crossword was just a wedding-gift idea Robby gave guests. The final clue read *"chatty or talkative"* = **GABBY**. Other clues included *"Lyreb"* (non-word, stumped her).
- **Pro-monogamy stance:** Robby is openly skeptical of open relationships. Her framing: *"99% of open relationships are bad."* Ties this to a [[themes/orthodox-upbringing|kosher-as-restraint]] argument.
- **Anti-pressure stance:** Robby argues nobody should feel pressured to marry — but if you're going to do it, mean it. The pressure norm is *"a Catholic Church holdover."*
- **One-hot-one-smart** is the operating model for her marriage. See [[bits/one-hot-one-smart]].
- **First "I'm falling in love with you"** came 13 days into dating Gabby.
