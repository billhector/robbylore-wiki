# Unentitled

[[people/robby-hoffman|Robby]]'s **first sold show** — an HBO project titled *Unentitled*. The sale came in the wake of her [[career/wga-strike-speech|2023 WGA strike-floor speech]]: per Robby on GQ × [[people/john-mulaney|John Mulaney]], it was *"how I sold my HBO show, Unentitled, in the end."*

## Key Takeaways

- HBO sale; first show Robby sold under her own name.
- Causally tied to the WGA-strike speech — the booing turned into industry attention, which turned into the deal.
- Distinct from the earlier [[career/hbo-script-sneak|HBO Canada $1,000 option]] of her courier-impersonation script.

## Sources

- [Comedian Robby Hoffman, One of the Funniest People Living, Interviewed by Her Friend, John Mulaney](https://www.gq.com/story/robby-hoffman-interviewed-by-john-mulaney) — GQ, December 12 2025; Robby names *Unentitled* as the show the WGA speech ultimately sold.
