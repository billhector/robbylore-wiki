# The Traitors (US)

Peacock reality competition series. [[people/gabby-windey|Gabby Windey]] **won Season 3**, cementing her reality-TV résumé before marrying [[people/robby-hoffman|Robby]] in January 2025. The Scotland-shoot production posture is one of the most-anecdoted slices of the Robby + Gabby relationship.

## Key Takeaways

- **Gabby won Season 3** of *The Traitors* US — her biggest reality-TV win, on top of finishing 2nd on *Dancing with the Stars* S31 and co-leading *The Bachelorette* S19.
- **Filmed in Scotland.** Production isolation: contestants weren't allowed unrestricted phone access; Gabby got **one supervised weekly call** with Robby, with the show's producer in the room. Robby's framing (per the Interview-magazine Lovebirds piece, Feb 2025): the constraint felt like prison.
- **Gabby cried every week.** *"So cold, nothing to eat, challenges really hard."* Robby coached her through the call's ~10 minutes.
- **Shema prayer card.** Robby gave Gabby a small card with the **Shema** (Jewish prayer) for the shoot; Gabby said it every night. The card became a recurring marriage / [[themes/orthodox-upbringing|orthodox-upbringing]] artifact across multiple sources.
- **The post-Traitors arc** included the Vegas elopement after the LA fires (Jan 11, 2025) — the marriage announcement broke just as Traitors S3 was airing.
- **Robby framing of Gabby's win:** *"The Bachelorette, Dancing with the Stars, Traitors — that's the order. She's been crushing reality TV her whole life."*
