# Long Winded

Podcast **hosted by [[people/gabby-windey|Gabby Windey]]**. [[people/robby-hoffman|Robby]] is the recurring guest / co-star — the joint episode titled *"The Robby Hoffman"* (released around [[projects/wake-up|Wake Up]] Dec 2025) is the deepest dual-perspective interview between the two on tape.

## Key Takeaways

- **Gabby's main podcast vehicle post-reality-TV.** Source of much of the Gabby material that didn't fit *The Bachelorette* / *Traitors* press cycles.
- **"The Robby Hoffman" episode** (Dec 2025) is the deepest dual-perspective interview between Robby and Gabby on tape — sourced for:
  - [[career/vegas-elopement|Vegas elopement]] details from Gabby's side.
  - [[people/uncle-eddie|Uncle Eddie]] folklore via Gabby's questioning.
  - [[people/nathan-letovsky|Nathan Letovsky]]'s Paris-painting-contest anecdote.
  - The vintage-shopping ritual; [[projects/wake-up|Wake Up]] release framing.
- **Domestic-dynamic on tape:** Long Winded normalized the *"my wife is the famous one and I'm the other comedian"* framing for Gabby — flipping the Bachelorette dynamic where Gabby was the spectacle.
- **Press-tour stop** during the Wake Up cycle and the Hacks / Dying for Sex simultaneous-streaming press wave.
