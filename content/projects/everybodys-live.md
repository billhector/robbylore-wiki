# Everybody's Live With John Mulaney

[[people/john-mulaney|John Mulaney]]'s Netflix late-night/variety series. [[people/robby-hoffman|Robby]] appeared in **May 2025** — alongside **Andy Samberg**, **Ramy Youssef**, and AAA media-relations manager (and former TV news anchor) **Aixa Diaz**, on a preselected-topic episode about the REAL ID rollout.

## Key Takeaways

- Robby entered the stage from the wings; within seconds blasted REAL ID as *"a money-grab by AAA"* and *"an attack on the poor, like everything else is."*
- Vaped on camera; passed the vape to Samberg (who puffed); offered it to Diaz, who declined even after Robby explained *"It's not nicotine."*
- Exited her seat by **giant-stepping over Mulaney's couch arms** to avoid disturbing Ramy Youssef and Mulaney himself, then jumped off and stuck the landing.
- GQ later called it *"an electric, wide-open couple of minutes."*

## Sources

- [Comedian Robby Hoffman, One of the Funniest People Living, Interviewed by Her Friend, John Mulaney](https://www.gq.com/story/robby-hoffman-interviewed-by-john-mulaney) — GQ, December 12 2025; the *Everybody's Live* segment is the opening anecdote.
