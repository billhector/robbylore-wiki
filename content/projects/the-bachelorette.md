# The Bachelorette

ABC reality dating series. [[people/gabby-windey|Gabby Windey]] **co-led Season 19 (Fall 2022)** with Rachel Recchia in a controversial dual-Bachelorette format. Her *Bachelorette* run is the public-eye context from which she met [[people/robby-hoffman|Robby]] roughly six months later.

## Key Takeaways

- **Co-lead of Season 19** with Rachel Recchia (the dual-Bachelorette format was unprecedented and divisive within Bachelor Nation).
- **Gabby got engaged** at season's end to **Erich Schwer** — Gabby's framing on Bethenny Frankel (Sept 2023): she chose not to sleep with every contestant, only one. The engagement ended ~6 months after the finale.
- **The dual-Bachelorette dynamic** unintentionally elevated Gabby — Bachelor Nation grew protective of her over Rachel during the season, and that loyalty carried into her post-show career. Robby's framing on Bethenny: *"They've treated Robby like she's one of us — that's like the best feeling ever."*
- **Was on Season 26 of *The Bachelor* first** — Gabby was a contestant on **Clayton Echard's** season before being upgraded to co-Bachelorette; Clayton called her *"the funniest person on the planet"* during a hike (cited by Bethenny Frankel as the gateway moment).
- **Bachelor Nation acceptance of Robby:** Gabby's audience absorbed her relationship with Robby relatively cleanly when announced August 2023 — Robby on Made It Out (Jul 2024): *"Gabby's fans are so excited, and they treat me well — shout out to Gab Nation, you've embraced me."*
- **Anchors the "former Bachelorette" framing** Robby uses in stand-up.
