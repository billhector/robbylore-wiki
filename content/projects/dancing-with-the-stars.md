# Dancing with the Stars

ABC competition show. [[people/gabby-windey|Gabby Windey]] competed on **Season 31** (Fall 2022) following her [[projects/the-bachelorette|Bachelorette]] season; **finished second place**. The DWTS run is the middle entry in Gabby's reality-TV résumé that runs Bachelorette → DWTS → [[projects/the-traitors|Traitors]].

## Key Takeaways

- **Gabby finished 2nd** on Season 31 (Fall 2022) — Gabby's framing on Bethenny Frankel (Sept 2023): *"I was second on Dancing With the Stars, Bethenny — I do know this."*
- **Where the Bachelorette-relationship ended:** per Gabby on Bethenny Frankel, the DWTS shoot was when she and **Erich Schwer** (her *Bachelorette* engagement) realized they weren't compatible in the real world — *"we were in the real world and now we're just figuring out we're not a match."* The popular *"Dancing-with-the-Stars-Curse"* didn't apply, she argues; it was just timing.
- **DWTS tour aftermath** — Gabby went on a post-show **tour** with the DWTS cast: *"I was literally in and out of YMCAs, living on a tour bus… I thought I was gonna die."* The tour fatigue is where she **questioned her sexuality** by watching *Perfect Match* on the bus with cast — the prelude to meeting Robby at Semi-Tropic in May 2023.
- **Hair-color autonomy battle:** Bachelor Nation production wouldn't let Gabby dye her hair while filming. DWTS gave her more leeway, which she leaned into as part of the *"I'll always change it"* identity assertion.
