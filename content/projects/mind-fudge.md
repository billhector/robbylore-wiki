# Mind Fudge

Web/streaming series **co-created by Justine Nelson** (2018–2019). [[people/robby-hoffman|Robby Hoffman]] wrote on **Season 2** (2019).

## Key Takeaways

- **Writer credit:** Robby on Season 2 (2019). Per the 2019 Joyful Magpies interview with co-creator Justine Nelson, the show ran an indie / web-series production posture, not a network commission.
- **One of four simultaneous 2019 writing credits** for Robby: [[projects/sherwood|Sherwood]] (2 eps), [[projects/odd-squad|Odd Squad]] (Daytime Emmy that year), [[projects/working-moms|Working Moms]] (13 eps), and Mind Fudge S2. This was the **breakthrough writers'-room year** that closed her pre-on-camera period.
- **Production context:** indie/web — predates the formal-network credits that would follow with [[projects/hacks|Hacks]] and [[projects/dying-for-sex|Dying for Sex]] in the 2020s.
- **Format:** short-form / streaming sketch-narrative hybrid in the same indie-Canadian-comedy lineage that produced Robby's earliest Toronto opportunities.
