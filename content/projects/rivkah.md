# Rivkah

[[people/robby-hoffman|Robby]]'s autobiographical series in development at **Showtime**. Title takes her given name, **Rivkah** (Yiddish form of Rebecca), which her family still uses.

## Key Takeaways

- **Showtime pilot** — development announced as early as **June 2021** ([Deadline coverage](https://deadline.com/2021/06/robby-hoffman-comedy-rivkah-showtime-development-1234769176/)).
- Still listed as in-development in the Spring '24 Interview magazine feature.
- Title doubles as identity declaration — the Rifka/Rivkah → Robby split is at the center of the autobiographical material.
- Drawn from the same well as the [[projects/hacks|Hacks]] Randi character but as Robby's own vehicle.
- **Producer cameo:** during a Bustle Frogtown lunch interview in **October 2023**, Robby and the journalist were seated next to **the actual producer of the Rivkah pilot** (in a *Weeds* hat and *Desus & Mero* jacket). Robby riffed about Addison Rae wanting a branded yarmulke for the show's promo. The encounter happened during the final weeks of the WGA strike — which technically restricted Robby's ability to talk to the producer about the project.
