# Rivkah

[[people/robby-hoffman|Robby]]'s autobiographical series in development at **Showtime**. Title takes her given name, **Rivkah** (Yiddish form of Rebecca), which her family still uses.

## Key Takeaways

- In development at Showtime as of the Spring '24 Interview magazine feature.
- Title doubles as identity declaration — the Rifka/Rivkah → Robby split is at the center of the autobiographical material.
- Drawn from the same well as the [[projects/hacks|Hacks]] Randi character but as Robby's own vehicle.

## Sources

- [Robby Hoffman on Synagogue, Small Talk, and Psychoanalysis](https://www.interviewmagazine.com/culture/robby-hoffman-on-synagogue-small-talk-and-psychoanalysis) — Interview, Spring 2024; first public mention of the series.
