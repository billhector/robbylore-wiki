# I'm Nervous

Robby Hoffman's **first stand-up comedy special** (2019), **recorded at the Just For Laughs comedy festival in Toronto for Crave TV** (per Wikipedia / Forbes 2019). Same year as her [[projects/odd-squad|Odd Squad]] Daytime Emmy win.

## Key Takeaways

- **Format:** one-hour stand-up special.
- **Recorded at:** Just For Laughs Toronto, 2019.
- **Distribution:** Crave TV (Bell Media's Canadian streaming service).
- **2026 Juno Award nomination — Comedy Album of the Year** (announced 27 January 2026 per CBC Music). The ceremony takes place at Hamilton's TD Coliseum on **March 29, 2026**, hosted by **Mae Martin**. Co-nominees in the category:
  - *Dragonflies* — Adam Christie
  - *Fish From the Jar* — Charlie Demers
  - *Dawud* — Dave Merheje
  - *Homesick.* — Faris Hytiaa
  - *I'm Nervous* — Robby Hoffman
- Robby on GQ × Mulaney: *"That was in Canada. Doesn't even count. It's like having a special in Brazil. Who cares?"* — i.e., she does not consider it equivalent to [[projects/wake-up|Wake Up]]. The Juno nomination is, in retrospect, a complication of that dismissal: the Canadian comedy establishment was paying attention.
- Bundled with the 2019 Daytime Emmy as the **breakthrough year** before her US-side career took off.
