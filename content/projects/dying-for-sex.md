# Dying for Sex

FX limited series (streaming on Hulu). [[people/robby-hoffman|Robby]] plays **"G"** — a *"sexual guide"* to Michelle Williams' character — in the episode **"Topping is a Sacred Skill"** (per Wikipedia + Robby's bio on My Favorite Lyrics with Devon Walker, Dec 2025).

## Key Takeaways

- **Character name:** **"G"**.
- **Episode:** **"Topping is a Sacred Skill"** (per Wikipedia).
- **Robby's role:** sexual-guide character to Michelle Williams' lead.
- **Premiered Spring 2025** — by April 13 2025 the show was actively streaming when Robby appeared on Lovett or Leave It with [[people/rachel-bloom|Rachel Bloom]] (who is also in the show).
- **Tag-team press with [[projects/hacks|Hacks]]:** the two shows landed within weeks of each other, giving Robby a simultaneous-streaming-on-Hulu-and-Max moment that anchored the May 2025 press wave (Fitzdog Radio, Lovett or Leave It).
- **The Robby–Rachel Bloom overlap** is the cleanest crossover moment between Robby's TV work and the broader Crazy-Ex-Girlfriend-adjacent Jewish-comedy world.
