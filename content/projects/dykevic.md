# Robby Hoffman Consulting Group

A live weekly call-in **advice show** hosted by [[people/robby-hoffman|Robby Hoffman]] on [[people/neal-brennan|Chris Gethard]]'s comedy network ***Planet Scum Live*** (2020–2021). Originally titled **Dykevic** (also rendered "Dykevice" in some sources). Predates the [[projects/too-far|Too Far]] podcast in Robby's hosting timeline.

## Key Takeaways

- **Format:** live, weekly, call-in. Robby gave on-the-spot advice.
- **Original title:** *Dykevic* (per Wikipedia filmography table) / *Dykevice* (per Vulture). Renamed to **Robby Hoffman Consulting Group** during the run.
- **Network:** [[projects/the-chris-gethard-show|Chris Gethard]]'s *Planet Scum Live* — Robby's first hosting gig after the Gethard writers-room phase.
- **Period:** 2020–2021 (pandemic-era).
- **Continuity:** the advice-show muscle here shows up later in Robby's persona on [[projects/too-far|Too Far]] and in her podcast-guest pattern of giving structured advice to callers (cf. [[projects/blocks|Block, Delete, Ignore, Ghost]] caller segments, [[projects/long-winded|Long Winded]] appearances).
