# The Rooster

**HBO** 10-episode comedy series. **[[people/steve-carell|Steve Carell]]** stars as **Greg Russo**, a fiction author who turns his life around to become a writer-in-residence at the same New England college his daughter Katie ([[people/charly-clive|Charly Clive]]) works at as a professor.

Created by **[[people/bill-lawrence|Bill Lawrence]]** and **[[people/matt-tarses|Matt Tarses]]**.

[[people/robby-hoffman|Robby]] plays **Mo**, the roommate of **[[people/lauren-tsai|Lauren Tsai]]'s Sunny.**

## Key Takeaways

- **HBO 10-episode series**; new episodes Sundays at 10 p.m. ET.
- Robby's role was originally **one day on set**; expanded into multiple episodes once the writers' room saw what she could do.
- Carell on the experience: *"It just felt like a true ensemble… it reminds me of my experience on The Office in terms of that."*
- Robby on Carell: *"I've botched it every time that I said hi to him, because it's impossible not to be starstruck with him, and he's such a classy man."* Finally broke and said *"My brother loved you in The Office."*
- The job sits squarely inside Robby's 2025–2026 breakout cluster — [[projects/wake-up|Wake Up]], [[projects/hacks|Hacks]] Emmy nom, The Rooster.
