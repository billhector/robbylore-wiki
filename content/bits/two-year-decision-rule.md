# The Two-Year Decision Rule

[[people/robby-hoffman|Robby]]'s rule for permanent body decisions: **tattoo = one-year wait, surgery = two-year wait.** You don't get to make either decision impulsively.

## The rule

- *"Tattoo = one year. Surgery = two years."*
- Applied directly to her [[career/top-surgery|top surgery]] — she waited the full two years between deciding she wanted it and scheduling it.
- Functions as a public-facing piece of Robby's general anti-impulse posture; sits next to the [[bits/no-backsies-they-them|"no backsies" rule for they/them pronouns]].

## Why it matters

- The rule is Robby's diplomatic shorthand when the conversation drifts toward gender/identity culture-war questions — she's pro-deliberation without being prescriptive.
