# One Hot, One Smart

[[people/robby-hoffman|Robby]]'s most-quoted relationship rule. **Every relationship needs one hot person and one smart person.** That's the rule. How those two attributes get split between the two people in the relationship is up to them.

## The bit

- *"Per every relationship you need one hot person, one smart person. How you divvy it up I don't give a fuck."*
- Robby's own arrangement with [[people/gabby-windey|Gabby]]: Gabby is 90% the hot one; smarts are split 50/50.
- **JLo/Ben failure mode:** Robby cites the JLo / Ben Affleck split as the canonical "missing a whole circle" — both attractive, but the smart side of the Venn diagram never closed.

## Why it works

- Universalist rule disguised as a hot take. Lands every time.
- Lets Robby brag on Gabby's looks while staying inside her butch-spokesperson register.
- Pairs with the [[themes/dating|"I am the boy in the relationship"]] identity claim — Robby self-identifies as the smart slot in her own marriage.
