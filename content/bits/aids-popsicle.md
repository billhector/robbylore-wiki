# The AIDS Popsicle

A [[people/robby-hoffman|Robby]] childhood bit. As a kid around age 10, she ate a Rocket popsicle, saw a red dot in the red layer that looked like a blood clot, and decided it was AIDS-infected — that "some psychopath" had loaded the popsicle factory with a syringe the way the playground rumor said they did movie-theater seats. She convinced her mother to buy **ten boxes** so she could cut up each red one and check.

## Key Takeaways

- Origin story for the "Robby is a child who reads the World Book and brings the mother daily research" persona.
- The mother actually bought the ten boxes — the bit lands as much on Robby's persistence as on the paranoia.
- *"That type of paranoia, early."*
