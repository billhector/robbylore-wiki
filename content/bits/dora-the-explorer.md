# "Dora the Explorer"

The opening line [[people/robby-hoffman|Robby]] used on [[people/gabby-windey|Gabby]] when they met. Gabby — fresh off [[projects/the-bachelorette|The Bachelorette]], age 28 — described herself as "exploring" her sexuality. Robby's reply: *"Exploring? What are you, Dora the Explorer?"*

- Sets the tone of their dynamic: Robby cutting Gabby's earnestness with a sharp comic deflate.
