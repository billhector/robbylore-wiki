# "No backsies on they/them"

Recurring [[people/robby-hoffman|Robby]] bit: once you've taken **they/them** pronouns, there's "no backsies." Frames her own non-trans queerness, [[career/top-surgery|top surgery]], and the politics of pronoun fluidity from the inside.

## Key Takeaways

- Robby has [[career/top-surgery|had top surgery]] but **does not identify as trans** — she frames it as gender dysphoria, not transition.
- The "no backsies" framing positions pronoun adoption as a one-way door, with comic finality.
- Recurring across podcast appearances; appears in stand-up rotation.
