# John Mulaney

Comedian, writer, director. Directed [[people/robby-hoffman|Robby Hoffman]]'s Netflix special [[projects/wake-up|Wake Up]] (Dec 14, 2025). Long-time Robby champion.

## In Robby's world

- **Directed [[projects/wake-up|Wake Up]].** Per Robby, "begged" to direct it once Netflix got interested.
- After Robby's [[career/wga-strike-speech|2023 WGA strike-floor speech]], Mulaney called it *"one of the greatest show-business debuts in history."*
- Robby cites Mulaney as the friend / industry advocate who routes opportunities her way.

## In Robby's house

- A **signed copy of his Netflix special *Baby J*** sits in Robby's house, photographed in the FT 2026 profile.
