# Schmuly Hoffman

[[people/robby-hoffman|Robby]]'s older brother. Works as a **mall security guard in Canada.** Robby's most-frequently-quoted sibling.

## Key Takeaways

- **Mall security guard** in **Saskatchewan, Canada** (per Live From Bed, Aug 2025) — *"He's like, 'Keep my name out your mouth. I don't need people knocking on my door. I live a private life — as if anyone — but as if anyone.'"*
- **Pro-German-Shepherd for LA:** Schmuly periodically calls Robby + Gabby to insist they upgrade from their Labradoodle (*"useless, runs up to everyone, zero barking"*) to a German Shepherd because *"you live in LA, it's a dangerous place."*
- **Calls [[people/gabby-windey|Gabby]] "toots."**
- **Tim Dylan superfan** — Robby's anecdote of him being delighted to hear of [[people/rob-reiner|Rob Reiner]]'s death turned on the fact that the news came from his idol [[people/tim-dylan|Tim Dylan]].
- **[[people/steve-carell|Steve Carell]] (Office) superfan** — the one fan Robby caved on while filming [[projects/the-rooster|The Rooster]]. *"My brother loved you in The Office."*
- **Also a Chris Gethard Show watcher** — was there for early visits to the writing room.
- **Shop-class wooden dildo** — at the Lubavitch boys' shop class, Schmuly built a wheeled wooden dildo (defined head, ridges, four small wheels). Robby's mother still **displays it on her piano** decades later. Robby uses the anecdote (Anniewood, Jul 2024) to argue mother's "but the shop teacher made him do it" defense of male teachers undercuts her current scaremongering about trans shop teachers.
- **Tank-top homophobia** — called teenage Robby a slur for wearing a tank top once. Robby's retroactive verdict: *"By then I'd given six handjobs — so he wasn't entirely wrong about the implication."* (Made It Out, Jul 2024)
- **On Robby's top surgery (Bein' Ian, Oct 2024):** *"What's this, you're cutting your tits off?"* — but not full opposition; the topic is allowed at the dinner table, unlike in their Catholic-equivalent neighbors who *"don't talk about shit."*
