# Schmuly Hoffman

[[people/robby-hoffman|Robby]]'s older brother. Works as a **mall security guard in Canada.** Robby's most-frequently-quoted sibling.

## Key Takeaways

- **Mall security guard** in Canada.
- **Calls [[people/gabby-windey|Gabby]] "toots."**
- **Tim Dylan superfan** — Robby's anecdote of him being delighted to hear of [[people/rob-reiner|Rob Reiner]]'s death turned on the fact that the news came from his idol [[people/tim-dylan|Tim Dylan]].
- **[[people/steve-carell|Steve Carell]] (Office) superfan** — the one fan Robby caved on while filming [[projects/the-rooster|The Rooster]]. *"My brother loved you in The Office."*
- **Also a Chris Gethard Show watcher** — was there for early visits to the writing room.
