# Jen Statsky

Co-creator of HBO's [[projects/hacks|*Hacks*]], with [[people/lucia-aniello|Lucia Aniello]] and [[people/paul-w-downs|Paul W. Downs]]. **Co-wrote the Randi role for [[people/robby-hoffman|Robby]]** — built around Robby's ex-Lubavitch, Crown-Heights, Montreal-raised background.

## Key Takeaways

- **Third member of the Hacks creator trio** that built Randi around Robby's specific bio. Per the Guardian, May 2026: the role's first line — *"Last week, I was a Hassidic Lubavitch Jew living in Crown Heights, New York. Now I'm in LA, I'm gay and probably an atheist"* — is essentially Robby's actual arc compressed into a Randi monologue.
- **Casting frame** Robby cites repeatedly: *"Had they cast anyone else, they would have been cancelled."*
- **Statsky-Aniello-Downs trio** is one of the densest comedy-writing partnerships in the post-2020 prestige-comedy era; in Robby's career, they're the people who used the *"writing-to-the-actor"* approach that Robby positions as career-saving.
- **The 6-lines / Emmy-nom paradox** — Randi appears in roughly 6 lines of dialogue in Robby's first Hacks season but earned a Primetime Emmy nomination (Guest Actress in a Comedy Series, 2025).
