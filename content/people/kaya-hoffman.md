# Kaya Hoffman

[[people/robby-hoffman|Robby]]'s **oldest sister**. Lives in **Scottsdale, Arizona** with husband **Bruno**. The "kept it neat" sibling; gave Robby her first set of sheets.

## Key Takeaways

- **Oldest of the ten Hoffman siblings.** The de-facto co-parent during the Montreal years.
- **Husband Bruno.** Scottsdale.
- **Flew in for Robby's [[career/losing-the-emmy|Emmy night]]** and marched her out of the ceremony after Robby lost — *"We don't need this shit. I came all the way from Arizona for this."* Robby came back anyway to present Cinematography.
- **Gave Robby her sheets** as a housewarming when Robby moved into her first apartment.
- **Possibly the sister also called Chaya** in some retellings — sources call her both names; treat as same person until clearer.
