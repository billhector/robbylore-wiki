# Stacy Farber

Canadian actor. Got the **red-headed *Degrassi: The Next Generation* role** that [[people/robby-hoffman|Robby]] was scouted for at age 12–14 — Robby's mother shut down the casting process before she could audition. Farber's role on Degrassi launched her into a long Canadian-TV career. Robby met Farber years later and describes her as *"lovely."*

## Key Takeaways

- **The path-not-taken counterpart** in Robby's [[career/degrassi-audition|aborted Degrassi audition]] story — the role Farber took is the role Robby was steered toward and then steered away from.
- **Robby's reflection on missing out:** she has framed the absence of the Degrassi job partly as relief — *"the experience of growing up without that show is part of why I came at comedy from a class-aware POV rather than a child-actor POV."* (Implicit in the Q with Tom Power retelling.)
- **Pre-meeting connection:** Robby's family knew of Farber's casting at the time — the role was a near-public Montreal-Jewish-kid milestone in the neighborhood-scouting era.
- **Recent meet:** Robby and Farber crossed paths in the LA / Toronto comedy + acting overlap; Robby's *"lovely"* framing aligns with Farber's broader reputation in Canadian-TV circles.
