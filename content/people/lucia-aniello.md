# Lucia Aniello

Co-creator of HBO's [[projects/hacks|*Hacks*]], with [[people/paul-w-downs|Paul W. Downs]] and [[people/jen-statsky|Jen Statsky]]. **Wrote the Randi role for [[people/robby-hoffman|Robby]]** — the part that earned Robby her [[career/losing-the-emmy|2025 Emmy nomination]] for six lines of dialogue.

## Key Takeaways

- One of three Hacks creators who built the Randi character around Robby's background.
