# Steve Carell

Actor and star of HBO's [[projects/the-rooster|*The Rooster*]] — created by [[people/bill-lawrence|Bill Lawrence]] and [[people/matt-tarses|Matt Tarses]]. Carell plays **Greg Russo**, a fiction author who becomes writer-in-residence at the New England college where his daughter Katie ([[people/charly-clive|Charly Clive]]) teaches. Robby plays **Mo**, roommate to [[people/lauren-tsai|Lauren Tsai]]'s **Sunny**. Carell calls the production reminiscent of *The Office* in ensemble feel — relevant given Robby's brother's fandom.

## Key Takeaways

- Lead of [[projects/the-rooster|The Rooster]]; Robby's role expanded from one day to many on-set days.
- Source of one of Robby's rare broken no-fanning-rule moments — *"I just literally had to say, 'Hey, my brother loved you in The Office.'"*
- Robby's running characterization, per Guardian May 2026: *"a doll."*
- Carell on the ensemble: *"It just felt like a true ensemble… it reminds me of my experience on The Office in terms of that."*

## In Robby's world

- Robby's role on [[projects/the-rooster|The Rooster]] was originally a one-day part; it expanded into many more days on set, which Robby attributes to her chemistry with Carell.
- Her brother [[people/schmuly-hoffman|Schmuly]] is a *huge* Carell fan, specifically for The Office. Robby caved on her usual no-fanning rule and told Carell, *"My brother loved you in The Office."*
