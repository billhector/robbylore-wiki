# Rachel Bloom

Comedian, singer, writer, and creator/star of **Crazy Ex-Girlfriend** (The CW). Released the Netflix stand-up special **"Death, Let Me Do My Special"** in 2025. Married, with a young daughter. Appeared with [[people/robby-hoffman|Robby]] as joint guest on [[projects/long-winded|Jon Lovett]]'s *Lovett or Leave It* Passover-themed episode (April 13, 2025), recorded during the *Hacks* + *Dying for Sex* simultaneous-press window.

## Key Takeaways

- **Married, *"the boy in the relationship"*** — used the same phrase Robby uses about herself. Robby: *"Wait, but you're the boy in the relationship?"* Rachel: *"Cuz I'm the boy in mine."*
- Released **"Death, Let Me Do My Special"** on Netflix (2025).
- Had a daughter mid-COVID; remembers re-taking press photos in the backyard at 5-months-postpartum because *"the world was falling apart but I needed new photos."*
- Robby and Rachel each pinged each other on which Jewish stereotypes are okay to lean into, and re-litigated marriage stages, [[projects/dying-for-sex|Dying for Sex]], and inanimate-object-pronoun gendering.
- Wore a Z-Bar shirt to the taping. Robby identified the typography from a partial-letter sliver: *"I can see even just the top of the ZBA. You see my autism?"*
