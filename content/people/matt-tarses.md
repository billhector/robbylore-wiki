# Matt Tarses

Showrunner / writer. Co-created [[projects/the-rooster|*The Rooster*]] with [[people/bill-lawrence|Bill Lawrence]] for HBO — the 2026 [[people/steve-carell|Steve Carell]] vehicle in which [[people/robby-hoffman|Robby]] plays **Mo** as a recurring role across 8 episodes.

## Key Takeaways

- **Co-creator of [[projects/the-rooster|The Rooster]]** with [[people/bill-lawrence|Bill Lawrence]].
- **Background:** longtime TV writer / showrunner whose previous credits sit in the same network-comedy tradition as Lawrence (Scrubs / Ted Lasso lineage) — Tarses is part of the broader Lawrence-network operating system that produced *The Rooster*.
- **Robby's casting trajectory:** her single-day appearance in the writers' room expanded into multiple episodes once the show's creative team saw her work. Tarses and Lawrence built the [[people/lauren-tsai|Sunny]]-and-**Mo** roommate dynamic around that expansion.
