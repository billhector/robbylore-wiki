# Jordan Jensen

Stand-up comedian based in New York. Co-host (with Ian Fidance) of **Bein' Ian with Jordan**, recorded out of Ian's downtown apartment. Also hosts her own solo show titled **RIP Jordan Jensen** (the "RIP" is a comedy-stage pun on *killing* a set, not a memorial — Jordan is alive). Masculine-presenting woman, biker, boxer, carpenter — describes herself as someone who *"fucks men but doesn't like that they hurt my feelings."*

> [!note] Podcast title clarification
> Jordan's solo podcast is titled "RIP Jordan Jensen." The title is a comedy-industry joke (R.I.P. = "killed" = a great set); Jordan was still actively touring and recording as of Robby's appearance on Ep. 60 in March 2026.

## Key Takeaways

- **Co-host of Bein' Ian with Jordan** (podcast w/ Ian Fidance). Recorded out of Ian's downtown NYC apartment since 2007.
- **Heated gender-semantics debate with Robby** on Ep. 115 (Oct 2024). Position: as masculine-presenting women, Robby and Jordan should both **stay identifying as women** rather than transitioning or going non-binary — *"a woman doing these things shows being a woman can look like this."* Jordan's framing crystallizes: *"some women are abandoning being a woman because being a man is better."*
- Friction with Robby on **top surgery** — Jordan was a flat-chested woman who didn't get top surgery and read Robby's choice through her own physical experience. Robby's reply: *"You can extend that grace to somebody who's had so many thoughts about wanting tits."*
- **Politics:** broadly aligned with Robby — anti-DNC, pro-Bernie, sees gender-identity discourse as elite distraction from class issues. *"Capitalism is the seed."*
- **Mutual friend with Robby via [[people/annie-lederman|Annie Lederman]].** Robby and Jordan had performed at the same venues for years but had not actually spoken before the Ep. 115 recording.
- **Romantic life:** describes herself as repeatedly hurt by men; toyed with the idea of joining a polycule. Talks about being chronically un-passed-at the Cellar despite booking elsewhere.
- **Reconciled with Robby on RIP Jordan Jensen Ep. 60** (Mar 2026). Both addressed the "(perceived) beef" from Bein' Ian Ep. 115 and agreed it had been a productive disagreement, not a real falling-out. Jordan brought Robby on to discuss OCD, success, confidence, self-worth, and safety while touring — Robby was on a Wake-Up press surge that included The View and Seth Meyers the same week.
- **OCD self-id** — Jordan repeatedly checks the stove before leaving the house, calls the Uber, gets back in to check the stove again, then has to explain to the Uber driver.
- **Chronically late** (in contrast to Robby's chronic earliness). Robby's framing of the dynamic: *"I like the dynamic between a late and an early person. It creates a good tension."*
