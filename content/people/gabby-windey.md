# Gabby Windey

Television personality and ICU nurse; wife of [[people/robby-hoffman|Robby Hoffman]]. Came up through reality TV ([[projects/the-bachelorette|The Bachelorette]], [[projects/dancing-with-the-stars|Dancing with the Stars]]) and won [[projects/the-traitors|The Traitors US Season 3]]. Hosts the [[projects/long-winded|Long Winded]] podcast.

## Key Takeaways

- Eight-year ICU nurse before TV — Robby's running line: *"She's practically a doctor in this country."*
- **Ex-Denver Broncos cheerleader** (Realtor.com, March 2025); also a former Bachelor and Bachelorette contestant.
- Met Robby **outside Semi-Tropic** (LA lesbian-night event) in **May 2023**; Robby was leaving as Gabby was filing out. Gabby was 28 and "exploring." Robby's opening line: *"Exploring? What are you, [[bits/dora-the-explorer|Dora the Explorer]]?"*
- Robby's full Interview-magazine framing of Gabby's attraction: *"She has to get the gab for real. She talks, she throws it back. As hot as she is, she doesn't need to be as funny and as smart as she is."*
- First substantive question Robby asked her: *"Do you come from money or not?"* Gabby grew up military.
- 13 days into dating, Robby told Gabby she was falling in love with her.
- Robby [[bits/proposing-via-crossword|proposed via a custom crossword]] (built by David Kaplan in Toronto); they eloped in [[career/vegas-elopement|Vegas]] with the $799 chapel package after fleeing the LA fires.
- Won [[projects/the-traitors|The Traitors]] US S3.
- Mexican heritage. Family is military. Mother helps with the LA house. Brother stays with Robby and Gabby.

## In Robby's bits

- The constant referent of [[bits/one-hot-one-smart|"one hot, one smart"]] — Robby's framing: Gabby is 90% the hot one; smarts are split 50/50.
- *"Practically a doctor"* — Robby's go-to one-liner about Gabby's nursing background.
- Domestic anecdotes: cooking oats for stomach aches, coordinating a [[people/dog-nardo|Nardo the dog]] near-death scare, broken-family conflict aversion.

## Habits + recent

- **Recently became an avid reader** (started "maybe two years ago" per FT 2026). Robby gave her an early edition of Sylvia Plath's *The Bell Jar* and plans to add a book per year to build a collection.
- **Separate bathrooms** in their LA rental — Gabby is serums-and-potions, Robby is one toothbrush.
- **No kids planned.** They eat out heavily — *"we live in restaurants."* Robby: *"This is my baby and I'm her baby. We're taking a page from the gays: Double income, no kids."*
- **Gabby's framing of the Vegas wedding** during the LA fire evacuation: *"If everything burns down, I just want to be married to you."*
- **Traitors filming protocol:** weekly **supervised** phone call with the show's producer in the room — Robby compares the constraint to prison. Gabby cried every week ("so cold, nothing to eat, challenges really hard"); Robby would coach her through 10 minutes.
- **Shema prayer card:** Robby gave Gabby a card with the Shema (*"Hear me"*) for the Traitors shoot in Scotland. Gabby said it every night.
- Considering conversion to Judaism — *"I say I will over Zoom."*
- **Lamictal + Gabapentin + occasionally Valium** as stabilizers (per Interview, Feb 2025).
- **Sleeps Porky-Pig-style** (big shirt, no underwear) to Robby's boxers.
- Has compared Robby to **Bob the Drag Queen** as the only person she'd previously met with comparable energy.

## Home

The LA rental — **Spanish-style, three-bedroom, one-bathroom, $5,000/month**, between Glendale and Pasadena. Moved in **July 2023**, one month into dating. Turquoise kitchen cabinets, black-tile bathroom backsplash, enormous soaking tub. Cozy fireplace, expansive dining room, private outdoor lounge.
