# Steph Tolev

Canadian comedian (also calls herself American-Canadian). **Bill Burr Presents: Friends Who Kill** Netflix-special alum; Vulture-listed *"Comedian You Should and Will Know"*; appeared in Bill Burr's *Old Dads*. Hosts the long-running **Steph Infection** podcast. Has lived a substantial chunk of her career in close orbit with [[people/robby-hoffman|Robby]] — they were **roommates** in Los Angeles for a period before Robby met [[people/gabby-windey|Gabby]].

## Key Takeaways

- **Former roommate of Robby** in LA. Steph took Robby in **after Robby's big breakup** (pre-Gabby). Robby slept in Steph's old bedroom; rent was around **$800/month** at Robby's choice — *"I think it was like $800, and then you went to do Iowaska."*
- **The roommate-breakup:** Robby moved out **without telling Steph** while Steph was on a 2-day trip to Joshua Tree. Steph found out via a mutual friend texting her on the trip. Steph's framing: "you can't tell someone on a trip, like if somebody is seizing you can't wake them up." Robby's framing: she'd found a great apartment that had to be jumped on. They were not friends for a stretch afterward.
- **The "hello while I'm napping" earlier fight:** when Steph was working a $7/hr babysitting job in LA and napping mid-day between gigs, Robby would loudly announce "Hello!" every time she came home, which broke Steph's nap. Steph claimed Robby kept yelling until she got a response. Massive fight; recurring bit on both their podcasts.
- **Reconnected and remain close** as of 2026. Steph hosted Robby on **Steph Infection Ep. "Speed Walking Until 93"** (Feb 2026) post-Wake-Up release; Robby in turn hosted Steph on [[projects/too-far|Too Far Pod]] (Aug 2024) and they did a joint episode framed as *"Robby's Former Roommate (Ft. Steph Tolev)"*.
- **Career notable:** Steph's Bill Burr *Friends Who Kill* spot was the inflection that pushed her past 50K followers — Robby tracks this with her own follower-growth memory: *"the friends-who-kill-Bill-Burr-thing got me really going."*
- **Steph's "spiral clip"** with a heckler got her another 80K follower bump — referenced by Robby as the only one of Steph's individual clips that produced a comparable single-jump effect.
- **Tour discipline parallel:** both Robby and Steph keep rigorous Instagram posting schedules. Robby has hers written out **three weeks in advance** with alarms for each post time; Steph manages a similar Australia-routed tour through Melbourne / Brisbane / Sydney spring 2026.
- **Robby cites Steph as a "mentor of kinds"** for her — Steph is a few years ahead in the working-comedian phase.
