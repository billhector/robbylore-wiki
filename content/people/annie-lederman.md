# Annie Lederman

Stand-up comedian and host of **Anniewood Podcast** (recorded out of Bad Ladder Studio). One of [[people/robby-hoffman|Robby]]'s recurring podcast collaborators — Robby has appeared on Anniewood three times that we know of, including Ep. 89 ("Buried Alive," July 2024), Ep. 20 ("Is Top Surgery Right For You"), and Ep. 127 ("Robby Hoffman has HUGE NEWS"). Annie also surfaces as a **mutual friend** in cross-podcast conversation — Robby and [[people/jordan-jensen|Jordan Jensen]] established their friendship via her on the Bein' Ian podcast (Oct 2024).

## Key Takeaways

- **Anniewood Podcast** is recorded at Bad Ladder Studio — Robby's running joke about the place: *"the cheapest studio in Hollywood,"* *"run by drug addicts."*
- Annie's twin brother is named **Todd**; the two share a birthday (mid-July).
- Annie keeps a lot of reptiles and pets in glass terrariums in her apartment, plus a Labradoodle — Robby called the place *"abuse for the dog"* and *"a level of clutter"* she could fix with Marie Kondo. Annie and Robby agreed to **turn a Marie Kondo session into Patreon content.**
- Annie pet-corrected herself when Robby objected to *"Todd and me"* / *"me and Todd"* grammar: Annie's response — *"language is devolving."*
- Annie was a Quaker; *"in my school everyone was a lesbian."* Her first-grade teacher died of AIDS.
- Annie's tour-friend orbit includes Ian Fidance, Jordan Jensen, Steph Tolev, Whitney Cummings (per Robby's *"Whitney had a really funny joke once…"* asides).
- Annie was **turning 41** the week the "Buried Alive" episode dropped (July 2024).
- **First Robby appearance on Anniewood: Ep. 20** ("Is Top Surgery Right for You?", March 2023) — pre-Gabby era. The on-camera top-surgery reveal (Robby topless; Annie matching with sports-bra-on partial reveal) is the episode's centerpiece. Recorded in Ybor City, Tampa.
- **Tesla owner** — Robby's running takedown of Annie's Tesla as a Steve-Jobs-vs-Elon-Musk-derivative-design product (vs. Robby's 1983 Toyota Corolla); cf. *"Steve Jobs would roll over if he saw that you just tapped like an iPad onto the dash and it wasn't integrated."*
- **Twin brother Todd** = Annie's podcast producer (Walker Media, @toddwalker.mp4). The Robby-on-Anniewood episodes repeatedly become Robby + Annie ganging up on Todd.
- **Goal:** an Anniewood/Robby Marie Kondo session for Annie's apartment, planned for Patreon.
- **Attended Robby + Gabby's Vegas wedding** (Jan 11, 2025) — confirmed on Anniewood Ep. 127 (Apr 2025): *"Robby Hoffman, fresh from the gyno, the blowing up, the new television star… her wedding in Vegas we attended."*
- **Twin brother Todd's birthday** is the same week as Annie's (mid-July) — they throw a joint birthday week in Irvine + Phoenix.
