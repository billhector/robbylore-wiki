# Neal Brennan

Comedian, writer (*Chappelle's Show* co-creator), and host of the **[[projects/blocks|Blocks]]** podcast — a long-form interview show structured around the *"blocks"* (psychological / experiential barriers) a guest works against. Brennan's interview with [[people/robby-hoffman|Robby]] during the [[projects/wake-up|Wake Up]] lead-up is the **densest single source on her [[career/top-surgery|top surgery]] arc** and the [[bits/two-year-decision-rule|two-year decision rule]].

- **Blocks format:** Brennan asks each guest to name their personal "blocks" upfront — the format lends itself to substantive psychological and biographical material rather than press-tour-banter. Robby used the depth to walk through top surgery in more detail than on any other appearance.
