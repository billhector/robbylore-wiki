A **Labradoodle**, **nearly nine years old**, originally [[people/gabby-windey|Gabby]]'s before she moved in with [[people/robby-hoffman|Robby]]. The defining domestic-life background presence in Robby + Gabby's 2025–26 press appearances.

- **Age:** nearly nine (as of 2025).
